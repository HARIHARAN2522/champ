# Workspace
Manage your projects and teams from a single dashboard.

This is where you create, organize, and control access to your workspaces. Whether you’re working solo or leading a team, the Workspace Manager gives you full visibility and control over who has access to what.

## Workspace Manager
The Workspace Manager lets you create, view, and manage all your workspaces in one place.

1) Create a New Workspace
2) Click **+ Create Workspace** in the top-right corner.
3) Enter a name for your workspace.
4) Click Create.
That’s it. Your new workspace is ready to use.

## Manage Existing Workspaces
You can invite team members or delete a workspace directly from the list.

To invite someone, click Invite next to the workspace.
To remove a workspace, click Delete and confirm.

>Warning: Deleting a workspace is permanent. All data inside it will be lost.

## Workspaces Tab
This tab shows every workspace you own or have been invited to.

Table Columns
- Workspace Name: The name you gave the workspace, such as CloudMaSa or ProjectX-Dev.
- Admin: The user who created the workspace.
- Email: The admin’s email address.
- Created: The date the workspace was set up.
- Actions: Buttons to Invite new members or Delete the workspace.

> Example Entry:  
> Workspace Name: CloudMaSa  
> Admin: admin  
> Email: admin@cloudmasa.com  
> Created: 12-11-2025

## Invitations Tab
Use this tab to track pending invitations and manage team access.

### No Pending Invitations
If you see “No pending invitations,” that means:

You haven’t sent any invites yet or All previous invites have been accepted or declined.
>Pro Tip: Click Invite next to any workspace to send a new invitation.

Best Practices
Follow these tips to keep your workspaces organized and secure:

- Use clear, descriptive names like Production-Infra or TeamA-Dev.
- Assign roles: Admin, Editor or Viewer based on responsibility.
- Review team members and permissions regularly to maintain security.
>Pro Tip: Use Masa Bot to automate workspace setup and team management tasks.
